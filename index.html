In a nutshell Design Thinking is about: Taking a human-centered approach to generating innovative ideas and solving problems.

Design Thinking is a tool that only graphic and industrial designers can use in the workplace: This statement is false because Design Thinking approach can be used by anybody in any sector to solve problems in the workplace, in the home and in the community.

David Kelley, Co-Founder of the IDEO design firm coined the term, “Design Thinking” in 2003
